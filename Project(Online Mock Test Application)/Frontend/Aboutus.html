About Us

Nag’s Academy: Mastering Aptitude & DSA

At Nag’s Academy, we specialize in the two most critical components of modern recruitment: Logical Reasoning (Aptitude) and Technical Efficiency (DSA). Our curriculum is designed to sharpen the mind and optimize the code, ensuring our students stand out in competitive, high-stakes environments.

1. The Aptitude Power-House

Most recruitment processes begin with an aptitude filter. We train students not just to solve problems, but to solve them with speed and precision.

Quantitative Ability

Master the math behind the logic. From Number Systems to Time & Work and Probability, we focus on shortcuts and mental calculation techniques.

Logical Reasoning

We build the ability to identify patterns. Our modules cover Blood Relations, Syllogisms, Seating Arrangements, and Data Interpretation.

Verbal Reasoning

Enhancing comprehension and professional communication to ensure clarity in every interaction.

Timed Mock Tests

Weekly “Pressure Tests” that simulate real company exam environments such as TCS NQT, AMCAT, and CoCubes.

2. The DSA Mastery Track

Data Structures and Algorithms form the foundation of efficient software. We move beyond syntax to teach strategy.

Module A: Foundational Data Structures

  • Linear Structures: Arrays, Strings, Linked Lists, Stacks, Queues
  • Non-Linear Structures: Trees (Binary, BST, AVL), Graphs, Hashing

Module B: Algorithmic Thinking

  • Sorting & Searching: Quick Sort, Merge Sort, Binary Search
  • Advanced Techniques: Greedy Algorithms, Backtracking, Recursion
  • Dynamic Programming: Breaking complex problems into sub-problems

Module C: Competitive Coding & Optimization

  • Time & Space Complexity using Big-O notation
  • Real-world problem solving on LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks & HackerRank
